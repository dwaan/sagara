About Sagara

Sagara's journey embodies a dedication to innovation, tailored solutions, and environmental responsibility. From custom product development to the launch of advanced frameworks and mobile apps, our focus remains on efficiency and sustainability. Our technologies prioritize environmental consciousness, accessibility, and privacy. Committed to excellence, we continue to build a brighter future, one solution at a time.

2007

Xinix emerged from the visionary hands of tech professionals with extensive experience across diverse domains.

2008

Our collaboration with the Indonesian government has further honed our ability to deliver comprehensive, timely, and high-quality product outcomes. As a result, Xinix has honed its expertise in the effective utilization of open-source technology

2011

Xinix has made the strategic decision to establish PT Sagara Xinix Solusitama as a legal entity, adopting Xinix Technology as our primary identity. Our core focus remains on innovative technologies that cater to all client needs.

The formalization of Xinix Technology's office has enhanced our readiness to tackle various client challenges. Our new office environment fosters creativity and innovation in the development and implementation of projects tailored to our clients' needs. We have also initiated the adoption of Agile methodologies in our development processes to better identify client requirements, deliver superior solutions, and ensure on-time project completion.

Recognizing the importance of proximity and connectivity with our clients, we have relocated our office from an area outside Jakarta to Rasuna, Kuningan in Jakarta, to better serve and engage with our clients.

2014

Consistently growing and thriving, our business expansion necessitated/h3e relocation of our office to a more spacious and strategically located area in Tebet. In the same year, we embarked on the development of our in-house PHP framework, which now serves as the foundational cornerstone for all our project developments. With this framework in place, we've accelerated project delivery timelines. We also began introducing responsive design technology to our clients, offering comprehensive solutions that enhance website traffic.

As our business continued to flourish, we recognized the need for even larger office space, prompting our relocation to Mampang. We initiated a corporate culture shift by instituting Friday workshops, promoting the exchange of knowledge and expertise among our employees. This was a period of innovative leaps, with the launch of our Ark PHP framework that supports our agile methodology, and the beginning of our journey into mobile app development.

Building on our previous year's experience, we intensified our research and development efforts, yielding a collection of top-tier frameworks. This includes Bono, designed for PHP-based system development; Xin, tailored for hybrid mobile app development; and Naked, serving as our UI framework.

2022

Following an intensive phase of technological development, we redirected our business focus towards custom solutions. This allowed us to cater to clients with unique business processes. As our evolving focus began to expand, we ventured into new sectors such as multimedia, infrastructure, and online retail. In this transformative year, we underwent an identity shift, rebranding ourselves as Sagara.

As a testament to the evolution of our technologies, we launched updates to our framework, introducing Bono 2, Xin 2, and Naked 1. This period saw the introduction of several of our mobile applications, including 'Kamus Pelayaran,' 'Hi-Style,' 'Nahjul Balagha,' and 'Ilmu Tadjwid.'

Our business strategy became more refined, offering tailor-made solutions while internally restructuring and enhancing employee well-being. These changes enabled us to better serve our clients, with a focus on delivering optimal results.
